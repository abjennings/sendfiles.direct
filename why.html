SendFiles.direct FAQ

Why?

The existing solutions for transferring files between computers weren't convenient enough. They all seem to require logging into an account or transferring a long, cryptic code.

Why not login to an account? (Email, Dropbox, etc.)

My passwords aren't memorized. They're in a password manager, so it's quite inconvenient to log in on another computer. Also, I may not trust the computer, so I don't want to type in an important password. And even if I did, it's annoying that the browser is going to remember my username and password unless I remember to wipe them afterwards.

What's wrong with a long, cryptic code? (Dropbox link, etc.)

I would need a good way to send the code to the other computer without logging in anywhere (see above), and I haven't found one yet. I could always write it down, but this is the 21st century. There has to be a better way!

How about a thumb drive?

If one of the computers isn't trusted, then I don't want to plug in a thumb drive. I've had a few fail on me, so I don't fully trust them. And sometimes I'm working via remote-access software (that doesn't support file transfer or clipboard sharing) and a thumb drive isn't an option.

Additionally, for small files and short distances, a thumb drive has quite a bit of annoying overhead (plug in the drive, wait for OS to recognize, open explorer/finder, copy files, unmount drive, unplug, walk across room, plug in the drive, wait 1-2 minutes for Windows to install drivers, open explorer, copy files, delete files, unmount drive, unplug).

So how does SendFiles.direct work?

  1. Open SendFiles.direct on one computer and scan the QR code on your phone.
  2. Drop the files you want to share onto the webpage.
  3. Open SendFiles.direct on the other computer and type in the computer ID shown on your phone.
  4. Download the files.

With an account ($5 for life), you never need to type the key. Just scan the QR codes on both computers to connect them.

Requirements

Chrome 31+ or Firefox 23+ is required, to support direct computer-to-computer connections (WebRTC).

Do my files go through your server?

No. SendFiles.direct uses WebRTC to transfer the files directly from one computer to another. However, a small amount of data is sent to a server to facilitate the connection.
